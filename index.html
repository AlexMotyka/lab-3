Perfect Steak

To cook a great steak, we recommend going to your local butcher for the highest quality of meat. Now we need to prepare the cooking surface, regardless of the method of cooking ensure they are HOT. The steak should be at room temperature and dry, rub oil on the steak preferably something with a high smoke point. Add salt and pepper as desired. When placing the steak on the pan or the grill place it gently away from you trying not to move it much. For a rare steak flip if just before the juices start coming out the top, for a medium-rare steak when the drops start to come up and a medium well when the juices start to pool. Once flipped keep the steak there for the same amount of time. Once you take it off the pan let it rest covered for up to 10 minutes this is too allow the juices to flow back into the steak. Then cut and serve!

List of things you'll need:

  • A great cut of steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Avovado oil
  • Pan or a grill
